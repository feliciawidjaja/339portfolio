About Me

Hello! I'm Felicia Widjaja, a third-year student at Simon Fraser University and a passionate UX/UI designer. I fell in love with the intersection of computer science and visual design, and since then, I've been dedicated to creating interfaces that are not only pleasing to the eye but also intuitive and user-friendly.

I strongly believe that the key to successful design lies in understanding the end-user. That's why my design process is deeply rooted in user research and usability testing. My designs go beyond aesthetic appeal; they aim to provide effective and enjoyable user experiences.
